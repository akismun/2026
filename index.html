Introduction

Welcome to our Model United Nations program, where students become global leaders in action. Here, delegates debate, negotiate, and solve real-world issues, building skills in diplomacy, public speaking, and strategic thinking. Through dynamic conferences and engaging committee sessions, participants explore diverse perspectives, learn to build consensus, and lead with confidence. Our MUN empowers students to think critically, act responsibly, and make a meaningful impact on the world—today and tomorrow.

Message From Director

Welcome to AKISMUN 2025. As we come together for this year’s conference, I encourage all delegates to approach each discussion with curiosity, creativity, and a willingness to think differently. Model United Nations is more than a simulation — it’s a chance to explore real-world challenges through fresh ideas and innovative thinking.

- ADAM RAZAQ
